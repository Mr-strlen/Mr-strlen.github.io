    • Recent Projects

    I have been working on the analysis of brain networks. From the mesoscale and single-cell scale, we constructed the connectivity of brain regions or neurons and performed network analysis. Simulations are also later performed on the networks to investigate the relationship between structure and function of brain.

  • Brain Network Analysis

    • Project Page

    We compared the networks generated from the experimental data with the networks based on peters' rule and analysed the differences in their network properties, including degree distribution, traid census, cost, storage capacity, routing efficiency and community discovery based on the laden algorithm. Afterwards, pertubation was performed to whole neuron, axon, and dendrite with three operations: scale, prune, and delete bouton respectively, to investigate the change patterns in network properties.

  • Cell Pattern Analysis

    • At the same time, we are also making attempts to use the morphological features of neurons for classification work. Whereas the current cell type is based on soma location, we hope to find new ways of classifying in the feature space, for example using prevalence analysis.

    Research
